TeamSnap Onboarding Instructions for Oldham County Allstars

Pre-Onboarding Checklist

What to Bring:

  • Athlete's Device: If the athlete has a smartphone or tablet, please bring it along.
  • Parent/Caregiver's Device: A smartphone or tablet for parents or caregivers.
  • Access to Email: Make sure you can access your email account for verification purposes.
  • New Email Setup: If you or the athlete don't have an email account, consider setting up a free Gmail or Yahoo account beforehand.

Step 1: Download the TeamSnap App

For iOS (Apple) Devices:

  1. Open the App Store.
  2. Search for TeamSnap.
  3. Tap Download next to the TeamSnap app.

For Android Devices:

  1. Open Google Play Store.
  2. Search for TeamSnap.
  3. Tap Install next to the TeamSnap app.

Step 2: Sign Up or Log In

  1. Open the TeamSnap app.
  2. If you already have an account, log in. If not, tap Sign Up.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to create your account.

Step 3: Join the Team

  1. Check your email for an invitation to join the Oldham County Allstars team on TeamSnap.
  2. Open the email and click Accept Invitation.
  3. Log in to your TeamSnap account if prompted.

Step 4: Complete Your Profile

  1. Once you're in the team dashboard, tap your name to go to your profile.
  2. Add or update any personal information, including contact details and any medical info you think the coaches should know.
  3. Save your changes.

Step 5: Explore the App

  1. Schedule: Check upcoming games, practices, and other events.
  2. Messages: Communicate with the team or individual members.
  3. Availability: Mark your availability for upcoming events.
  4. Photos: View or share photos with the team.

Step 6: Additional Settings

  1. Tap the Settings icon (usually a gear).
  2. Here you can adjust your notification preferences, including how you'd like to be notified about upcoming events or schedule changes.
